Hummus + cilantro

A delicious traditional recipe, but jazzed up.

One can make their hummus as they see fit. I do not think I have ever made 2 the same.

    Ingredients
  • 1/4c. tahini
  • 1/4c. lemon juice
  • 1/4c. olive oil
  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 2 or 3 smashed garlic pieces
  • a small bunch of fresh cilantro (coriander)
  • Salt + pepper + turmeric
    Steps
  1. Blend oil, lemon juice, and tahini.
  2. Add chickpeas and remaining spices and ingredients. Blend.
  3. Taste test. Add a bit of water if too thick.
