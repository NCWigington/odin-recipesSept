Toum - Garlic Bomb

A delicious, if not powerful emulsion of garlic and oil. Like a bomb of flavour and garlic hitting your tastebuds. It takes a bit of time to emulsify the oil with the garlic, but it is so good... but maybe not too healthy...

  • 1c. garlic
  • 1/4c. lemon juice
  • 1.5c oil
  1. Blend garlic and half the lemon juice.
  2. Slowly add the oil to the blend to begin and maintain the emulsion.
  3. FInished product with be a white foamy mass that will kick up the flavor of any roasted meat.
